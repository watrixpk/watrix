Why Watrix

The design firm that should have existed three years ago

Pakistan is installing solar at one of the fastest rates in the world. Every megawatt faces the same challenge — soiling, water quality, and O&M costs that erode performance over a 25-year asset life. No engineering consultancy in Pakistan was purpose-built to solve this. Watrix is.

Specialist expertise, not a generalist afterthought
Water engineering is our entire focus — not a sub-service offered alongside electrical design. You get senior engineering attention on every project, every time.
Tender-ready deliverables, not rough drafts
Every document we produce is designed to go directly into a proposal or construction package — complete BOQs, stamped drawings, and O&M documentation included where specified.
International standards, local knowledge
All designs delivered to IEC, WHO, and NEPRA standards. Our team has worked on large-scale public health and infrastructure projects across Pakistan.
Fast turnaround, lean structure
A 5-business-day design SLA for standard scopes. No management layers between you and the engineer doing the work. No overhead passed on to clients.
